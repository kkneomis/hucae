CPA Boot Camp

The Howard University School of Business Center for Accounting Education and Becker CPA Review have entered into a partnership to provide a superior CPA review course for recent college graduates held at Howard University in May.

The seven-week course (five days per week) is multi-media (a mix of live and national instruction). Each participant receives copies of all course materials, including required textbooks and Passmaster/Simulation/Final Exam Software upon enrollment. Becker faculty will teach the program courses along with one HU Professor.

To maximize success, participants are advised not to work during the program (full-time or part time). Participants must be eligible to sit for the Exam and will have begun applying for their NTS prior to the course start date. ALL BOOT CAMP PARTICIPANTS ARE REQUIRED TO SIT FOR ALL FOUR PARTS OF THE CPA EXAM IN JULY AND AUGUST FOLLOWING THE COMPLETION OF THE BOOT CAMP. We hope that over 90% of the participants pass all parts of the exam. Before the completion of the program, each participant will need to supply CAE with the dates they will be taking each part of the exam.

Participants must have access to a computer for homework assignments, and will be required to provide the lead instructor with a copy of their performance summary weekly.
