The Center of Accounting Education is dedicated to

Awareness

Increasing awareness of the lack of blacks and minorities in the accounting profession.

Leadership

Providing leadership skills training programs that benefit minorities in accounting, from undergraduate to early career, and thought leadership papers that address the advancement of minorities and blacks in the accounting profession.

We encourage students, professionals, educators and others to use our resources, participate in our programs and contribute to the success of minority accounting students and young professionals nationwide.
