We're About Success

As part of CAE’s commitment to creating a program and environment which encourages the success of African Americans in the accounting profession, this program – We’re About Success! is considered our flagship. It provides the opportunity for participants to collectively discuss cross-firm challenges along with the choices and opportunities they may encounter in the accounting profession.

This annual 4-day program focuses on the young African-American professional with one year or less of experience in the accounting field. CAE hosts approximately 125 participants selected by our stakeholder firms and helps them learn essential skills for adjusting to and navigating the high-powered corporate environment.

Included during the week are sessions on:

  • Getting Off to the Right Start
  • Uncovering Unconscious Bias
  • How to Handle Difficult People & Situations
  • Building Support & Alliances
  • Self-Awareness

This program encourages and assists participants to sit for and pass the CPA Examination as early in their career as possible.

Additionally, through this Program, participants can expand their network…a key to future success.

This insightful week will include lively panel sessions and thought-provoking workshops to provide you with expert advice on achieving a successful career in the accounting profession.

CAE has a group of corporate stakeholders who contribute resources, expertise and experience in the field of accounting. Together we develop programs to ensure that these minority professionals receive the additional skills necessary to enable them to be successful.

We’re About Success! has grown significantly since its inception and is strongly endorsed by the leading accounting firms in the nation.

Over the course of these 4 days, participants are given the opportunity to enhance and expand their skills, build networks and share experiences.

Some of the accounting industry’s most influential people and firms facilitate sessions and participate in panel discussions during the week.
